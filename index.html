Art Fest
6-8 July

Art as a form of cultural expression

Music

In this time of uncertainty you can be 100% sure we’ll bring you the best music we possibly can. An eclectic mix of newcomers and well established bands, acoustic acts and solo performances, spread out throughout the city for all communities to enjoy. We’ll keep you posted.

Activities

You’ll be able to enjoy flea markets, music and vinyl fairs, showcases, drum camps and many more to be announced. You’ll also be able to relax and enjoy the sun with a cool summer cocktail if that’s your thing.
